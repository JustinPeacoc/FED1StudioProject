Class Subjects

Geometry

Introduction courses for Geometry. Covers the basics of geometry and how to apply geometry to everyday life as well as how to apply the knowledge to other math subjects.

Shapes and Polygons

Introduction courses to shapes and polygons. Learn to distinguish the differences between what a shape is and what a polygon is. What shape is a polygon?

Trigonometry

Introduction courses for Trigonometry. Apply functions to geometric problems.

Advanced Geometry

Advanced geometry class for those who excelled in Introduction to Geometry.

Advanced Trigonometry

Advanced trigonometry class for those who passed and excelled in Introduction to Geometry and Introduction to Trigonometry.

Algebra

Learn basic algebraic equations and formulas.
