About Us

Born in 2016, Poly Edu is a non-profit orginization based in Kansas City with a goal to educate people residing in low-income residential areas. We are a group of specialized individuals with a passion for educating people in a variety of subjects in mathematics, particularly geometry. At Poly Edu, our core principle is inclusion. Anyone of any background can come to this safe space to expand their knowledge and engage in their community.

Why Choose Us

Expertise

Our staff and instructors are people who have an impressive background in mathematics. Our hiring process is the most stringent and Kansas City so we deliver the best content and teaching methods to those who desire a more expansive knowledge in mathematics.

Pricing and Volunteer

We are a non-profit orginization so we don't ask students to pay any fees. We get funding from donors and we have volunteers to help bring this community a little further together. We also engage in volunteer work around the community to help make Kansas City a better place to live in for all of us.
